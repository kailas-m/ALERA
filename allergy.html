Common Skin Conditions

Rashes & Dermatitis

Inflammatory skin reactions caused by irritants, allergies, or infections. Includes eczema, contact dermatitis, and various rash types that cause redness, itching, and discomfort.

Moles & Lesions

Skin growths that can be benign or potentially malignant. Regular monitoring helps detect changes that might indicate skin cancer development.

Infections

Bacterial, viral, or fungal infections affecting the skin. Includes impetigo, warts, ringworm, and other contagious conditions requiring proper diagnosis.

Inflammatory Conditions

Chronic conditions like psoriasis, rosacea, and acne that cause persistent inflammation, redness, and skin changes requiring ongoing management.

Potentially Serious Conditions

Skin changes that may indicate serious health issues, including early signs of melanoma, basal cell carcinoma, and other skin cancers.
